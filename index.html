Welcome to VE6EVL's list of radio resources.

As a new ham, the thing I found most challenging was the overwhelming volume of available information. I would dive in and quickly get lost.

The intent of these pages is to try and provide some structure to that information, and once you've got the "NEED to know" stuff down, you can dive into other areas that may interest you.

This is an ever-evolving list I'll add to as I find more "good stuff" that I think is helpful.

Enjoy your journey! VE6EVL

  • The Basics - start here if you're new to / just learning amateur radio.
  • Supporting Resources - extra resources to help you learn. (Remember - amateur radio is a journey.)
  • Equipment - various radio manufacturers, online stores, applications, etc.
  • Specialites - there are MANY areas you can dive into once you're licensed.
  • Misc - things that are related to, but not specifically for amateur radio.
